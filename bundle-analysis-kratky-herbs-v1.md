## What This Bundle Is

Kratky is a passive hydroponic method. The plant sits in a net cup above a nutrient solution. Roots grow down into the solution while part of the root zone stays exposed to air. There is no pump, no electricity requirement, and very little maintenance if the reservoir is sized properly.

Best crops:

- Basil
- Mint
- Parsley
- Coriander, with caution because it bolts easily
- Chives, with caution because they are slower

Avoid for this bundle:

- Rosemary, lavender, woody herbs
- Large fruiting crops
- Herbs in hot direct sun without reservoir temperature control

## Product Decision Pass 1

File: `kratky-herb-product-decisions-v1.csv`

This first product pass separates confirmed direct listings from unresolved product decisions.

Confirmed direct listings:

- Reservoir quality path: Hydro Tank & Solid Lid - 39L
- Reservoir DWC-ready path: Hydro Tank & Lid with 200MM Hole - 39L
- Net cups: 40 x Mesh Net Pot - 2 Inch

Still unresolved:

- True budget reservoir fallback from a large retailer
- Rockwool cubes or grow sponges
- Budget pH test kit
- Herb seed pack
- Budget LED grow bar
- Matching hole saw

Important budget finding:

The quality-path bundle is already too expensive for a clean `<$100` promise if it uses a $54.95 hydro tank, $19.95 net cups, and a $74.95 nutrient. That product set is good, but it belongs closer to a “best quality path” or “project path”, not the cheapest plug-and-play path.

To keep the low-budget Kratky recommendation honest, we need two product paths:

| Path | Best For | Product Direction |
|---|---|---|
| Lowest-cost path | Low budget, DIY/project users | Dark lidded tote or jars, net cups, budget nutrient, pH test, seeds |
| Quality hydro path | Some Assembly / Likes A Project users who value reliability | Hydro tank, net cups, one-part hydro nutrient, pH test, seeds |

The quiz should not show the hydro tank path as a sub-$100 complete bundle unless we find cheaper nutrient and testing options.
