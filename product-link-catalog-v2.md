# GrowACrop Product Link Catalogue v2

Purpose: replace generic marketplace searches with specific, buyer-ready product listings. This version prioritises official hydroponic products and specialist hydroponics retailers. General hardware retailers should only be used when the item is genuinely generic, such as a drill, hole saw, measuring jug, or spray bottle.

## Product Selection Rules

- Prefer specific hydroponic listings over generic Amazon/eBay searches.
- Prefer named products with visible product photos and clear specs.
- Avoid recommending storage tubs as the main image/product for Kratky or DWC unless clearly labelled as a fallback DIY option.
- Use Amazon/eBay only when a named hydroponics listing cannot be found from a specialist retailer.
- Keep product links separate from recommendation logic so the quiz can later swap retailers without rewriting the matrix.

## Recommended Changes To The Quiz Product Data

1. Replace free-text shopping list links with product IDs.
2. Show “Recommended listing” as the main button and “Alternate” as a secondary link.
3. Add a small note when a recommendation is a parts bundle rather than a complete kit.
4. For fruiting crops, stop linking low-budget plug-and-play users to weak tiny systems. Show a warning and a stronger alternative such as AutoPot, a tent kit, or a real DWC/tank build.
5. Raise or split the fruiting crop premium budget. Real indoor fruiting kits often sit well above $600 once light, tent, ventilation, nutrients, and monitoring are included.
